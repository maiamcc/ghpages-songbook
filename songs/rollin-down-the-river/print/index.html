Rollin' Down the River

Jack Forbes (revised by various)

I once was a rigger and I worked like hell,

Rollin' up, rollin' down

And now I'm a sailor with the OCL.

And go rollin' down the river
Rollin' up, rollin' down, We'll all get drunk in Tilbury town. In twenty-four hours we'll turn around And go rollin' down the river.

Now the work is good and the wages fine,

Rollin' up...

When you make a trip in a container line.

And go rollin...

When first I saw a TEU
I wondered where they put the crew.

Now the cargo comes in TEUs—
That's a twenty-foot box, boys, full of booze.

There's a Tilbury girl called Kettle Jane;
It's her I think of every day.

She's got a friend called Teapot Anne;
They go out walkin' hand in hand.

The Tilbury girls go round in pairs,
And they don't give a damn about a sailor's wares.

The Tilbury boys look mighty swell;
To hell with the channel and the OCL!

Notes: OCL - Overseas Container Line

TEU - the twenty-foot equivalent unit is a general unit of cargo capacity
