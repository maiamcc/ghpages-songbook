Haul Away Joe

trad.

When I was just a little thing, my mama often told me

Away, haul away, we’ll haul away, Joe!

That if I were to kiss them all, I’d better kiss them boldly.

Away, haul away, we’ll haul away, Joe!
Away (HEY!), haul away, we’ll haul away together, Away, haul away, we’ll haul away, Joe! Away (HEY!), haul away, we’ll haul for better weather, Away, haul away, we’ll haul away, Joe!

I used to have an Irish man but he’s not into ladies,

Away, haul away…

But now I’ve got a Bristol lass and she calls me her baby,

Away, haul away…

Old Louis was the king of France before the Revolution,
But then he got his head chopped off, it spoiled his constitution.

Ya call yourself a “Second Mate,” ya cann’e tie a bowline,
And you can’t even stand up straight when the packet, she’s a-rollin’.

Well now can’t ya see, the black clouds are a-gatherin’?
Well now can’t ya see, the storm clouds are a-risin’?
