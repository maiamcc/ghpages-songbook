Magalina Hagalina

trad. American

Magalina Hagalina oaka naka walka talka Oaka noka polka was her name (clap clap)!

She had two hairs on the top of her head:
One was alive and the other was dead!

She had two eyes set deep in her head:
One was green and the other was red!

She had a big mole in the middle of her chin;
She said it was a dimple, but dimples point in!

She had two teeth in the middle of her mouth:
One pointed north and the other pointed south!

She had two holes in the middle of her noes:
One was open and the other was closed!

She had two hips like battleships:
One stayed home and the other took trips!

She had two feet, size eleven and a half:
One took a shower and the other took a bath!

(Etc.)

This is the end of Magalina’s song…
But you can find her on the internet at hag dot com!

(Repeat chorus ad nauseam, speeding up.)
