Cool of the Day

Jean Richie

My Lord, He said unto me:
“Do you like my garden so fair?
You may live in this garden if you keep the grasses green,
And I’ll return in the cool of the day.”

Now is the cool of the day (2x) Oh this earth, it is a garden, The garden of my Lord, And He walks in His garden In the cool of the day.

Then my Lord, He said unto me:
“Do you like my garden so pure?
You may live in this garden if you will feed my sheep,
And I’ll return in the cool of the day.”

Then my Lord, He said unto me:
“Do you like my pastures so green?
You may live in this garden if you will feed my sheep,
And I’ll return in the cool of the day.”

Then my Lord, he said unto me:
“Do you like my garden so free?
You may live in this garden if you keep the people free,
And I’ll return in the cool of the day.”

Notes: First recorded 1977
