Wayfaring Stranger

trad.

I am a poor wayfaring stranger,
A'traveling through this world of woe;
But there's no sickness, toil or danger
In that bright land to which I go.

I'm going there to see my Father, I'm going there no more to roam: I'm only goin' over Jordan, I'm only goin' over home.

I know dark clouds will gather 'round me;
I know my way is rough and steep;
But beautious fields lie just before me,
Where God's redeemed their vigils keep

I'm going there to see my mother; She said she'd meet me when I come. I'm only goin'...

I want to wear a crown of glory
When I get home to that bright land.
I want to shout Salvation's story
In concert with that bloodwashed band.

I'm going there to see my Savior; To sing His praise forevermore. I'm only goin'...
